OUR STORY:
TECHNICAL INTEGRITY SINCE 2004.

Climate Architect was born from a simple observation: Jacksonville’s homes needed more than just a man with a wrench. They needed precision engineering. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to radical transparency, Marcus Sterling founded this company to bring editorial-level quality to the HVAC industry.

As a locally owned and operated business, we treat every square foot of your home as if it were our own. We understand the specific demands that Florida’s humidity places on mechanical systems, and we’ve spent two decades perfecting the "Climate Blueprint"—our proprietary process for ensuring peak efficiency and surgical-grade indoor air quality.
